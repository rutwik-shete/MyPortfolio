## Time complexity

![Big-o complexity chart](/img/slack-imgs.jpg "Complexity chart")

- O(log n) - Binary search
- O(n) - Linear search
- O(n log n) - Sorting
- O(n^2) - ??
- O(n!) - Brute force: Going through every permutation. \_Number of nodes ^ Number - 1 ^ Number - 2 ...
